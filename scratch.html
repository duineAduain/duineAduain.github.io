Scratch

Scratch is a block-based programming language used to teach programming and computer science.

Scratch was created in 2003 by Mitchel Resnick of MIT Media Lab, John Maeda of MIT, and Yasmin Kafai of UCLA after they proposed the idea to the National Science Foundation and were given a grant to complete the project.
